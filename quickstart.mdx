## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

<Check>Sui wallet installed (Sui Wallet, Suiet, or Ethos)</Check>
<Check>$100 USD worth of PUMPKIN tokens</Check>
<Check>Exchange account (BloFin, Bybit, HTX, or WEEX)</Check>
<Check>Basic understanding of cryptocurrency trading</Check>

<Warning>
  Never share your API keys or wallet private keys with anyone. QuantumVoid will never ask for your private keys.
</Warning>

## Step 1: Get PUMPKIN Tokens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up Sui Wallet">
    Install a Sui wallet extension
  </Step>

  <Step title="Acquire PUMPKIN">
    Purchase PUMPKIN tokens to meet the minimum requirement:
    - **$100 USD worth** at current price
    - OR **2.8 million PUMPKIN tokens** (whichever is less)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Balance">
    Check your balance meets requirements before proceeding
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Connect to QuantumVoid

1. Navigate to trade.quantumvoid.org
2. Click **"Connect Sui Wallet"**
3. Approve the connection request
4. Sign the authentication message

## Step 3: Add Exchange Credentials

Choose your exchange and follow the detailed setup guide

## Step 4: Start Your Bot

1. Navigate to **Bot Management**
2. Select a preset configuration
3. Click **"Start Bot"**
4. Monitor in real-time
