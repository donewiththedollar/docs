## Access Check Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Connect your SUI, Solana, or EVM wallet to voidx.trade
  </Step>

  <Step title="Balance Verification">
    System automatically checks your PUMPKIN balance
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Granted">
    Any PUMPKIN balance > 0 grants Tier 1 access
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy Bots">
    Full access to bot deployment and management
  </Step>
</Steps>

- Early supporters
- VIP users

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much PUMPKIN do I need?">
    Any amount. Even 1 PUMPKIN token grants Tier 1 access to the full platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to keep PUMPKIN in my wallet?">
    Yes. The balance check happens when you connect and when starting bots. If your balance drops to zero, you'll lose access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use any chain?">
    Yes! PUMPKIN on SUI, Solana, or BASE all grant access. Use whichever chain you prefer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the higher tiers?">
    Higher tiers are coming soon and will unlock additional features and benefits for larger PUMPKIN holders. Details will be announced.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I'm whitelisted?">
    Whitelisted addresses bypass all PUMPKIN checks completely. No tokens needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
